About the Pasqal Community:

The Pasqal Community is the hub for quantum enthusiasts to shape the future of quantum computing. Whether you're a developer eager to craft the next corner stone of quantum algorithms, a researcher diving into uncharted territory, a student ready to deepen their understanding of quantum systems, or simply a curious mind – there's a place for everyone here.

What can you expect from the Community:

Learn about quantum computing through an interactive e-learning platform Connect with peers worldwide via a dedicated Slack workspace and exclusive events Access Pasqal open-source tools to run quantum computation. Collaborate with peers to co-create and have a direct impact on the future of quantum computing
